Communication Protocals

Transparency on what and how the team is working on their individual assignments.
Regular update messages need to be sent through slack to keep team informed of progress and changes.
Follow up,  Have another teammate check your work.
Everyone needs to use the To Do List so that we are all on the same page.  Check off items as you finish them.
In case of emergency please text or call the appropriate team member with the cell phone numbers provided google.docs.
Tuesday night meetings used for clarification of week's deliverables.
Thursday night meetings used for presentation of each team member's deliverables.
